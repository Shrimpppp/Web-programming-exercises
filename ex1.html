Cheese pizza recipe

A step-by-step recipe on how to make a cheese pizza.

Ingredients

the doughs ingredients

  • 1 1/3 Cups of warm water
  • 1 packet of active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups of All-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Cornmeal

Ingredients of the sauce

  • 1/2 cup of Pizza sauce
  • Mozerella cheese

Instructions

  1. In the bowl of a stand mixer, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast.
    Let it sit for 5 to 10 minutes until the mixture becomes foamy.

  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix on low speed using a dough hook until the dough comes together and pulls away from the sides of the bowl.
    Knead the dough on medium speed for 5 minutes, or by hand on a floured surface for 5–7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.

  3. Lightly grease a large bowl with olive oil. Place the dough in the bowl, covering it to prevent drying.
    Let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  4. Preheat your oven to its highest setting—typically 475°F to 500°F (245°C–260°C).
    If you have a pizza stone or baking steel, place it in the oven while it preheats. Divide the dough into two equal portions for two medium pizzas.

  5. On a floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or pan dusted with cornmeal to prevent sticking.
    Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce, then top with mozzarella cheese and your preferred toppings.

  6. Slide the pizza onto the preheated stone/steel in the oven (or place the baking tray in).
    Bake for 8 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  7. Let the pizza cool for 5 minutes before slicing.
